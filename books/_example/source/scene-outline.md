# Scene-by-Scene Outline — [BOOK_TITLE]

**Format:** Each chapter gets its own section with the following structure:
- **POV:** Whose perspective does the reader inhabit in this chapter?
- **When:** What is the time period? (Date, relative time, season, etc.)
- **Where:** What is the primary location?
- **Beats:** Break the chapter into 3-5 narrative beats. Each beat should be a meaningful unit of action, revelation, or emotional movement.
- **Turn:** What changes at the end of this chapter? What question emerges? What new information shifts the reader's understanding?

---

## Note on Development

This outline is a structural blueprint, not a prose outline. It should capture the *architecture* of the story — beats, turns, pacing — without prescribing exact dialogue or internal monologue. Use it to:

1. Track pacing across the full manuscript
2. Ensure each chapter does meaningful work
3. Map character arcs across chapters
4. Plan POV distribution (which characters do readers follow, when, and why?)
5. Identify structural turning points
6. Flag chapters that might be underperforming or redundant
